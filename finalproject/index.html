What does hiking mean to you?

Hiking is an activity that can be enjoyed by everyone, either solo, with family, friends, or with your favorite pet. A chance to get outside and relish the beauty of mother nature. Perhaps a stroll through a dense swamp, the open air of the sand dunes, or a strenuous hike up a steep ravine. Maybe you want to find a place of solitude, or a place to think about the many stresses of life, maybe forget about those stresses. Hiking can be very physically demanding or a simple stroll.

Michigan has more hiking trails than most states in the U.S. The vast terrain in northwest Michigan's lower peninsula has many opportunities to find just the right trail for you. Some of Michigan's trails have everything: dense wetlands, high dunes, fast rivers or slow streams. Find your passion and inspiration, it's right out your door.
